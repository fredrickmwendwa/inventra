How it works

From sign-up to selling, in minutes

01

Set up your business

Register your business and owner account. No paperwork, no waiting — your workspace is ready instantly.

business.name → "Acme Supplies Ltd."
owner.role → "owner"
workspace → ready ✓
02

Add your products

Import or manually add your catalog with categories, pricing, and opening stock. Set reorder thresholds once.

product.stock_quantity → 48
product.reorder_level → 10
status → "active"
03

Invite your team

Add staff with the right role. Managers can adjust stock and confirm sales; staff can record sales at the counter.

staff.role → "manager"
permissions → scoped
tenant → isolated ✓
04

Sell, track, repeat

Every confirmed sale deducts stock automatically. Your dashboard updates in real time — no manual reconciling.

sale.status → "confirmed"
stock_movement → "out"
revenue → +KES 4,200
